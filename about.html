About Godwin Dudu-Orumen

Godwin Dudu-Orumen is a man of uncommon abilities, a polymath whose talents span law, sports, and journalism. He is a double 1st degree holder in English Language and Literature and Law from the University of Benin, and also a graduate of the Nigerian Law School.

His commitment to any cause is one hundred percent. An individual who suffers no fools gladly, he speaks with boldness and candour to the Sports Establishment on matters of policy and conduct of administrators.
